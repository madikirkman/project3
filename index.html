Ed Sheeran

Albums through the years

Ed Sheeran is a singer-songwriter who was born in West Yorkshire and who grew up in Suffolk. He is currently 30 years old and one of the world's best-selling music artists. He has produced four albums named after math operations, which include "Plus", "Multiply", "Divide", and "Equals". His early success came from the song "The A Team" on his first album, and since then his popularity and success has increased dramatically.

"Plus" was Ed Sheeran's debut album and was released on September 9, 2011 through Asylum and Atlantic Records. Prior to this album, Sheeran had released five EPs independently, and this album was considered his breakthrough. The album has sold over 4 million copies. Influences for the album include rapper Eminem, Nizlopi, and Damien Rice. Sheeran has no band on the album and instead just uses an acoustic guitar.

"Multiply" was Ed Sheeran's second album. It was released on June 20, 2014 for Australia and New Zealand and worldwide on June 23, 2014 through Asylum and Atlantic Records. It reached No. 1 in 15 countries, including the US Billboard 200. The single "Sing" on the album was Sheeran's first UK number one song, while "Thinking Out Loud" became his biggest hit in the US at the time. The album went 4x Platinum and Spotify named it the most streamed album in the world in 2014.

❮ ❯

"Divide" was Ed Sheeran's third album and it was released on March 3, 2017 through Asylum and Atlantic Records. The album won the Grammy Award for Best Pop Vocal Album and in the United Kingdom was the fastest-selling album by a male solo artist. Sheeran went on a 260 show tour to promote the album, and the tour became the biggest, most attended, and highest-grossing tour of all time.

Released on October 29, 2021, "Equals" is Ed Sheeran's most recent album. It was also released through Asylum and Atlantic Records. It reached number one in 15 different countries and received mixed reviews from music critics. He described it as his "coming-of-age" album, and included songs about his marriage and the birth of his daughter.
